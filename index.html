Features

Built for travelers
with opinions.

We designed Wayfarer for people who know what they want — they just need the time and tools to find it.

◎

Preference-first recommendations

Not "Top 10 things to do." Instead: a guide filtered through your interests, dietary needs, comfort level, and the things you'd rather skip entirely.

⊕

Choose your sources

Trust Reddit locals over travel blogs? Prefer Yelp reviews to influencer guides? Toggle exactly which sources inform your itinerary.

◈

Conversational planning

Chat naturally with your planner. "No nightlife, more street food." "Can we do this in a half day?" It understands context and adjusts on the fly.

⟳

Automatic bookings

Once you approve the plan, we handle reservations — OpenTable, Resy, ticketing — so your calendar fills itself. No tab-hopping required.
